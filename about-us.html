Company Profile

Kishan Brass industries were incepted in the year 2010 with an objective Manufacturer and Supply Brass product. our primary vision is to supply quality precision brass components at the most competitive prices and side by side to improve employment to the people and some extended fulfilling the social responsibilities of the society. We are located at Jamnagar, Gujarat, India. Which is famous for its Brass products and known as “Brass City” which provides the perfect background for many industries. We have successfully attained a leading position, under the vigilance and guidance of the proprietor of the company Mr. Paresh Akabari Having experience of 8 years, he had managed to take the company at a leading position amidst State as well as National companies. Moreover, we are recognized as the most prominent Brass fitting parts manufacturers and among the Wash Basin Coupling exporters from all over India.

Infrastructure

Our factory covers an area of more than 4500 sq. feet buildup and is situated in the Jamnagar district in Gujarat. Our factory is equipped with advanced machines and facilities so as to meet the requirement of the customer. Apart from this, we also own a Row-material processing plant for the purpose of maintaining our quality standards.

Quality Control

Quality control Management is the chief strength of our organization. We manufacture of produce from IS grade raw material to provide our customers absolutes satisfaction in term of quality. We are having our own in-house laboratory to check up the size tolerance measurement of the product which we have manufactured in our factory with digital caliper vernier, micrometers for measuring, for threading inspection. other tests are also conducted as per requirements.

Network

Our well-developed network enables us in the procurement and delivery of brass products well within the stipulated time. this network is spread all over India.
